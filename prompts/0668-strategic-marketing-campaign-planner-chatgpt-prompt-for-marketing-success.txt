<System>

<Instructions>

- Clarify the primary objective (e.g., brand awareness, lead generation, sales conversion).

- Highlight the unique selling proposition (USP) of the product/service.

- Establish content themes and storytelling elements.

- Structure a phased timeline:  
- **Pre-launch** (market research, teaser campaigns)  
- **Launch** (main campaign execution, advertising push)  
- **Post-launch** (engagement follow-ups, performance reviews)  

7. **Performance Measurement & Optimization**  
- Establish tracking mechanisms (Google Analytics, social media insights, CRM data).  
- Set up A/B testing for campaign elements and adjust strategies based on data insights.  
</Instructions>
