Helpful Guide for Best Input

Visual Concept Variables

- Building Type: 
- Commercial: Skyscraper, corporate headquarters, high-tech research lab, data center, vertical farm, spaceport terminal.

- Residential: Apartment complex, smart home, arcology, self-sufficient dome city, floating city, orbital habitat.

- Public/Cultural: Art museum, concert hall, public library, memorial, transportation hub, vertical park.

- Conceptual: Space station, lunar base, underwater research lab, atmospheric refinery, interdimensional portal.

- Environment: 
- Urban: Dystopian megacity, sleek metropolis, overgrown post-apocalyptic city, neon-lit cyberpunk street, integrated with nature.

- Natural: Lush vertical forest, tranquil coastal region, desolate desert, snowy mountain peak, volcanic badlands.

- Extra-Terrestrial: Martian landscape, lunar crater, asteroid field, gas giant orbit, alien planet.

- Conceptual: Inside a nebula, floating on clouds, submerged in an ocean, within an alternate dimension.

Style Direction Variables

- Architect/Artistic Style: 
- Real-World Architects: Zaha Hadid (fluid forms), Santiago Calatrava (skeletal structures), Frank Gehry (deconstructivism), Tadao Ando (concrete and light), Renzo Piano (high-tech).

- Sci-Fi Aesthetics: Cyberpunk, Steampunk, Solarpunk, Dieselpunk, Dystopian, Utopian, Biomorphic, Parametric, Brutalist.

- Artistic Movements: Bauhaus (clean lines), Art Deco (geometric forms), Cubism (fragmented planes), Expressionism (emotional exaggeration).

- Feeling/Atmosphere: 
- Emotional: A sense of grandeur, futuristic serenity, quiet solitude, overwhelming awe, subtle mystery.

- Technical: Industrial, high-tech, technologically advanced, organic, clinical, retro-futuristic.

- Mood: Urban decay, peaceful tranquility, organized chaos, silent tension, vibrant energy.

Technical Parameters Variables

- Camera/Lens Combo: 
- Camera: Canon R5, Sony Alpha A7R V, Hasselblad X2D.

- Lens: 24mm tilt-shift lens, 85mm prime lens, wide-angle lens, fish-eye lens.

- Drone: DJI Inspire 3.

- Lighting: 
- Natural: Soft morning light, dramatic sunset, harsh midday sun, foggy atmosphere, clear starlight.

- Artificial: Volumetric lighting, neon glow, sharp high-contrast shadows, ambient occlusion, ray tracing.

- Effect-based: God rays, lens flare, caustics, cinematic bloom.

- Materials: 
- Metals: Polished chrome, brushed aluminum, oxidized steel, iridescent titanium, rusted iron.

- Composites: Self-healing polymers, graphene composites, translucent ceramic, reinforced carbon fiber.

- Stone/Concrete: Rough concrete, polished obsidian, liquid stone, glowing quartz.

- Organic: Living green facades, grown mycelium structures, crystallized biomechanical elements.

Composition Guidelines Variables

- Compositional Rules: 
- Standard: Rule of Thirds, Golden Ratio, Symmetrical Balance, Asymmetrical Balance.

- Advanced: Leading lines, dynamic diagonals, negative space, framing.

- Camera Angle/Perspective: 
- Low: Dramatic worm’s-eye view, low-angle shot.

- Mid: Eye-level view, straight-on view, close-up shot of a specific detail.

- High: Aerial view, bird’s-eye view, wide-angle establishing shot.

- Key Feature to Highlight: 
- Form: Monumental scale, unique curvature, geometric complexity, fluid organic shapes.

- Detail: Intricate facade, glowing details, complex structural elements, integrated landscaping.

- Atmosphere: Sense of isolation, technological advancement, integration with nature.

Exclusion Parameters Variables

- Specific Exclusions: 
- Human/Life: No human figures, no animals, no crowds.

- Technology: No standard cars, no visible power lines, no outdated technology.

- Style: No rust, no graffiti, no traditional building materials, no soft edges.

- Environment: No vegetation, no clouds in the sky, no ocean.
