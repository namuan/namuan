1. Strategic Campaign Development
Purpose: Generate comprehensive multi-channel marketing campaigns with tactical execution plans and performance projections.

<Role_Objective>

</Role_Objective>

<Context>

Target Audience: {target_audience}
Product/Service: {product_service}
Business Objectives: {business_objectives}
Timeline: {campaign_timeline}
Geographic Scope: {geographic_scope}
</Context>

<Instructions>
Using Chain-of-Thought reasoning, systematically develop:
1. Analyze target audience behavior patterns and media consumption habits
2. Identify optimal channel mix based on audience preferences and budget allocation
3. Create messaging hierarchy aligned with customer journey stages
4. Design tactical execution plan with specific deliverables and timelines
5. Establish KPIs and measurement framework for each channel
6. Develop contingency plans for different performance scenarios
</Instructions>

<Constraints>
- Stay within specified budget parameters
- Ensure legal compliance across all channels
- Maintain brand consistency throughout campaign
- Consider seasonal and market timing factors
- Account for competitive landscape dynamics
</Constraints>

<Output_Format>
Provide structured output with:
- Executive Summary (100 words)
- Channel Strategy Matrix (table format)
- Tactical Timeline (Gantt-style breakdown)
- Budget Allocation (percentage breakdown)
- Risk Assessment and Mitigation Plans
- Success Metrics Dashboard
</Output_Format>

<Reasoning>
Apply System 2 Attention to focus only on data-driven insights, removing subjective bias. Use Diverse Verifier approach to evaluate multiple strategic options before recommending optimal path. Employ Contextual Chaining to ensure each campaign element builds upon previous decisions.
</Reasoning>
