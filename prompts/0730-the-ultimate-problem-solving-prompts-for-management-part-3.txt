Set 3: Management Problem-Solving Prompts

Managing Resistance to Organizational Change

Employees are resisting a major change initiative, causing delays and friction. Develop a strategy to overcome resistance and ensure successful adoption of the change.  

Handling a Budget Shortfall in a Key Project

A critical project is running out of funds before completion. Identify cost-saving measures and alternative funding sources to keep the project on track.  

Preventing Burnout in High-Performing Teams

Your top employees are showing signs of burnout, affecting morale and productivity. Develop an intervention plan to prevent burnout while maintaining performance.  

Reviving a Declining Brand

Your company's market share is shrinking due to outdated branding and reduced customer engagement. Develop a revitalization strategy to make the brand relevant again.  

Handling Negative Employee Feedback on Glassdoor

Your company has received multiple negative reviews online, damaging its reputation as an employer. Develop a plan to address concerns and improve workplace satisfaction.  

Increasing Employee Retention in a Competitive Market

High employee turnover is affecting company stability and morale. Identify key reasons for attrition and create a retention strategy to attract and keep top talent.  

Recovering from a Failed Product Launch

A new product has failed to gain traction in the market. Analyze the causes and develop a recovery strategy to reposition the product or reallocate resources effectively.  

Managing Office Politics and Power Struggles

Internal power struggles are creating division among leadership and employees. Develop a strategy to foster collaboration and minimize the impact of office politics.  

Ensuring Compliance with New Industry Regulations

A change in industry regulations requires major adjustments in company operations. Create a compliance roadmap to ensure legal adherence without disrupting business functions.  

Handling a PR Crisis Involving a CEO Scandal

Improving the Effectiveness of Virtual Meetings

Virtual meetings in your organization are unproductive and disengaging. Develop a structured approach to make remote collaboration more effective and efficient.  

Restoring Trust After a Data Breach

Your company has suffered a cyberattack, compromising customer data. Develop a crisis management and trust-rebuilding strategy to reassure customers and stakeholders.  

Reducing Workplace Absenteeism

Frequent employee absenteeism is affecting productivity. Identify the underlying causes and create a policy to improve attendance and engagement.  

Handling a Merger or Acquisition Smoothly

Your company is merging with another, leading to uncertainty among employees. Develop a communication and integration strategy to ensure a smooth transition.  

Revamping an Outdated Sales Strategy

Your sales team is struggling to meet targets due to outdated techniques. Develop a modernized sales strategy incorporating digital tools and customer-centric approaches.  

Navigating Cultural Differences in a Global Team

Your team consists of employees from diverse cultural backgrounds, leading to misunderstandings. Develop a strategy to promote inclusivity and effective cross-cultural collaboration.  

Balancing Short-Term Goals with Long-Term Strategy

Your company is focused on immediate revenue but neglecting long-term sustainability. Develop a balanced approach to achieve both short-term and long-term success.  

Preventing Scope Creep in Projects

Project requirements keep expanding beyond the initial agreement, causing delays and resource strain. Develop a scope management strategy to maintain control and prevent overruns.  

Retaining Key Clients Amidst Increased Competition

Competitors are aggressively targeting your key clients. Develop a client retention strategy to reinforce relationships and maintain loyalty.  

Handling Media Scrutiny After a Workplace Incident

A workplace accident has attracted media attention, affecting company reputation. Develop a transparent communication plan to manage the situation responsibly.  

Managing Generational Differences in the Workplace

Different age groups in your workforce have conflicting work styles and expectations. Develop a strategy to bridge generational gaps and foster collaboration.  

Scaling Customer Support Without Losing Quality

As your company grows, customer support requests are increasing, causing delays. Develop a scalable solution to maintain high-quality support without overburdening the team.  

Handling Employee Backlash Over Pay Disparities

Employees have discovered significant pay gaps within the company, leading to dissatisfaction. Develop a strategy to address concerns and create a fair compensation policy.  

Revamping a Stagnant Company Culture

Employee engagement has dropped, and the company culture feels uninspiring. Develop an action plan to reignite enthusiasm and strengthen company values.
