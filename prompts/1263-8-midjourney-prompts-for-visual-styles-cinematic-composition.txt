How to Use These Prompts
Copy any prompt and paste it into Midjourney. The prompts include specific camera settings, lighting conditions, and style references. You can modify aspect ratios, stylization values, and version numbers based on your needs.
Change the --s values to adjust stylization:

- Lower values (50-200): More realistic

- Higher values (500-1000): More artistic

Adjust --ar ratios for different formats:

- 1:1 for square images

- 16:9 for wide compositions

- 4:5 for portrait orientation
