1. Friendly Animal Companion

Perfect for family brands, pet services, or companies wanting approachable, trustworthy vibes.
Prompt:
Create a friendly animal mascot character. Subject positioned in welcoming three-quarter pose with bright, cheerful expression, one paw/hand raised in greeting gesture, wearing simple branded accessory like cap or bandana. Composition features mascot centered with subtle background elements suggesting their habitat, clean negative space around character. Camera: 50mm lens equivalent, eye-level perspective, medium depth of field keeping mascot sharp, full-body framing. Lighting: soft even lighting with gentle key light from front-left, warm color temperature, subtle rim lighting for dimension, no harsh shadows. Style: clean cartoon aesthetic with friendly proportions, bright saturated colors, smooth gradients, approachable design language. No realistic textures, no aggressive expressions, avoid complex backgrounds, keep design simple and memorable.
