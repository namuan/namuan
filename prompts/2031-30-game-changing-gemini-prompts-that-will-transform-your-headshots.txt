Pro Tips That Make These Prompts 10x Better:
Before you copy-paste these prompts:

- Lighting matters: Take your source selfie in good natural light

- Angle up: Position your phone slightly above eye level

- Clean background: Start with an uncluttered space behind you

- High resolution: Use your phone’s highest quality camera setting

- Multiple tries: Generate 3-4 versions and pick the best one

Bonus hack: Combine prompts! Mix professional lighting with creative backgrounds for unique results.
