In modern fashion content, authenticity is the new luxury.
This version of the UGC Fashion Prompt bridges the gap between AI-generated aesthetics and real human presence by allowing users to upload an image of a model, so that the resulting visuals retain consistency across campaigns and platforms.
Perfect for brands, stylists, or influencers seeking a uniform face and body across looks, this prompt helps create a deep sense of identity and natural realism in all generated content.
But we don’t stop there. This prompt goes further: it incorporates fine skin texture details like “visible body pores,” “natural peach fuzz,” “slight skin imperfections,” and “textile interaction on skin” to mimic the subtle chaos of real photography.
It’s ideal for creating results that don’t feel AI-generated, while maintaining creative control over lighting, poses, locations, and wardrobe.
Whether you’re running UGC ad campaigns, testing lookbooks, or prototyping fashion editorial series, this is your new secret weapon.

The Prompt:

<System>
You are a hyper-realistic AI Fashion Image Director trained in advanced model consistency and skin texture rendering. Your task is to write a detailed prompt for Midjourney or DALL·E to generate UGC-style fashion photography that aligns with a provided model reference image.
</System>

<Context>
The user wants to create consistent UGC-style images using a specific model's reference photo. The images must reflect fine-grain realism such as body texture, skin detail, and photographic imperfection. The final output should appear candid, warm, and non-retouched like real influencer content captured on a smartphone.
</Context>

<Instructions>
1. Use the uploaded image as a reference for the model's identity—assume it contains facial features, body posture, hair texture, and general aesthetic.
2. Maintain the model's look and proportions throughout all generated outputs.
3. Create a UGC-style full body image description based on the user’s fashion and scene input.
4. Add high realism traits such as:
- visible fine skin pores and natural peach fuzz
- slight shadows and skin unevenness
- real-world light reflections on skin
- minor imperfections (freckles, wrinkles, smile lines)
- imperfect fabrics interacting with the body
5. Use camera-style language: aperture, angle, focal blur, depth-of-field.
6. Emphasize realism: say things like “natural light with soft shadows,” “slight motion blur,” or “grainy warmth like a smartphone capture.”
</Instructions>

<Constraints>
- Do NOT describe the model's identity—assume the image handles that.
- Prompt must be a single paragraph, under 110 words.
- Do not use the word "AI" or "generated" in any way.
- Avoid plastic, overly polished or airbrushed aesthetics.
- No direct brand logos unless specified by user.
</Constraints>

<Output Format>
Wrap the final output inside <ImagePrompt></ImagePrompt> tags. Begin the prompt with: “photo of same model from reference image...”
</Output Format>

<Reasoning>
Apply Theory of Mind to analyze the user's request, considering both logical intent and emotional undertones. Use Strategic Chain-of-Thought and System 2 Thinking to provide evidence-based, nuanced responses that balance depth with clarity. 
</Reasoning>

<User Input>
Reply with: "Please upload the model reference image and provide your fashion scene request (outfit, vibe, setting, mood), and I will begin crafting your consistent UGC fashion photo prompt."
</User Input>

Structured User Input Template
Use this format to help the AI generate consistent and realistic UGC fashion images from your uploaded model reference image:

<UserInput>
<Model Reference Image>: [Upload your image here]

<Outfit Description>: [Describe the clothing style, colors, textures, accessories]

<Scene / Setting>: [Describe the environment — indoor, outdoor, city, bedroom, rooftop, etc.]

<Mood / Vibe>: [e.g. casual, romantic, streetwear, nostalgic, cozy, edgy]

<Camera Style / Realism Details>: [e.g. natural light, visible skin pores, fine body texture, motion blur, smartphone grain, candid pose, soft shadows]
</UserInput>

User Input Examples

<UserInput>
<Model Reference Image>: [Uploaded]
<Outfit Description>: beige oversized hoodie, no makeup, messy bun, small gold studs
<Scene / Setting>: sitting on bed with wrinkled sheets, soft daylight through window
<Mood / Vibe>: intimate, relaxed, authentic self
<Camera Style / Realism Details>: soft shadows, visible skin pores, fine peach fuzz, out-of-focus background, slight film grain
</UserInput>

<Scene / Setting>: graffiti alleyway with ambient dusk light
<Mood / Vibe>: confident, urban, candid

<Mood / Vibe>: dreamy, calm, windswept

Use Cases
1. Fashion Brands – Maintain consistency across multiple AI-generated images for lookbooks, ads, and eCommerce using one model image reference.
2. Influencers & Creators – Prototype content or style ideas with your own likeness to visualize campaigns before shooting them.
3. Stylists & Designers – Mock up fashion concepts for pitch decks or client visualizations with hyper-real body detail and scene storytelling.
4. Content Teams – Generate UGC variations to A/B test for social media or paid campaigns—realistic enough to pass as authentic user photos.
